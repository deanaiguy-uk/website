Common Questions

Do I need any technical knowledge?▼
Not at all. Every session is designed for non-technical people. We meet you where you are and build confidence with practical, hands-on exercises — not theory lectures.
Can you come to our office or school?▼
Yes! All workshops and training sessions can be delivered on-site at your location in Sheffield and South Yorkshire. Remote options are also available for teams across the UK.
What AI tools do you use and recommend?▼
We're completely tool-agnostic. We recommend what works best for your situation — whether that's ChatGPT, Claude, Copilot, Gemini, or specialised tools for your industry.
Is there support after training?▼
Yes. All packages include follow-up support. The AI Kickstart includes 30 days of email support. Retainer clients get ongoing, dedicated support. We don't just train and disappear.
How do I know which service is right for me?▼
The easiest way is to book a free strategy call — 15 minutes, no obligation, no pitch. Or take our free AI Audit tool for an instant assessment of where AI can help most.
Are your school workshops safeguarding compliant?▼
Absolutely. Safeguarding and data protection are non-negotiable. All school-facing work follows current DfE guidance and we hold an enhanced DBS check. We provide policy templates and frameworks as standard.
